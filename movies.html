10. Den of Thieves 2: Pantera

This action-packed sequel to the 2018 heist thriller dives deeper into the intense rivalry between the elite LA County Sheriff’s Department and a highly skilled crew of bank robbers planning a massive heist across international borders.

  • Starring: Gerard Butler, O’Shea Jackson Jr., and 50 Cent.
  • Suitable For: Fans of action-packed thrillers with intricate heist plots and tactical combat sequences.
  • Streaming Service: HBO Max, Apple TV, Google Movies.
