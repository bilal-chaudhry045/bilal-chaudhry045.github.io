The Top 10 Books Of March 2025

Below we have compiled a list of the top ten Books of March 2025.

The list is numbered from 10-1, saving the best for last.

ENJOY!

10. Sunrise on the Reaping

This prequel to The Hunger Games series explores the origins of the dystopian society of Panem. Set during the 50th Hunger Games, it delves into the political machinations and societal structures that led to the creation of the brutal event, providing a deeper understanding of the Capitol‚Äôs control over the districts.

  • Written By:Suzanne Collins.
  • Suitable For:Fans of The Hunger Games series and readers interested in dystopian fiction.

9. Wild Side

The third installment in the Rose Hill series, this contemporary romance follows two characters navigating love and personal growth in a small-town setting. Their journey is filled with emotional challenges, personal discoveries, and the complexities of modern relationships.

  • Written by:Elsie Silver.
  • Suitable For:Readers who enjoy heartfelt romance novels set in charming small towns, focusing on character development and emotional depth.

8. The Fall Risk

A contemporary romance that intertwines themes of love, personal challenges, and unexpected connections. The characters face obstacles that test their resilience and commitment, leading to a heartfelt and engaging narrative.

  • Written By:Abby Jimenez.
  • Suitable for:Fans of contemporary romance who appreciate stories about personal growth, resilience, and the complexities of modern relationships..

7.Wild dark Shore

Set in a near-future dystopian world affected by climate change, this novel follows a family living on a remote island. As rising sea levels threaten their home, they must confront environmental challenges and personal dilemmas, leading to a poignant exploration of survival and resilience.

  • Written by:Charlotte McConaghy.
  • Suitable for:Readers interested in eco-fiction, dystopian narratives, and stories that explore human resilience in the face of environmental crises..

6. Our Infinite Fates

A speculative fiction novel that delves into themes of destiny, choice, and the interconnectedness of lives. The narrative explores how individual decisions can ripple through time, affecting not only the protagonists but also the world around them.

  • Written by: Laura Stevens.
  • Suitable For:Fans of speculative fiction and narratives that explore the complexities of fate, choice, and interconnected lives.

5.The Strawberry Patch Pancake House

Set in the cozy town of Dream Harbor, this novel revolves around a struggling pancake house on the verge of closing. A determined woman steps in to save it, and in the process, she rebuilds her life, finding love, friendship, and purpose along the way.

  • Written by:Laurie Gilmore.
  • Suitable For: Readers who enjoy heartwarming stories with themes of community, second chances, and personal growth.

4. Dream Count

A literary novel that weaves together the lives of individuals across continents, tackling themes of migration, identity, and the passage of time. Through interconnected narratives, it explores how dreams, aspirations, and personal struggles shape destinies.

  • Written by :Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie.
  • Suitable For:Readers who appreciate literary fiction that delves into complex themes of identity, migration, and personal growth.

3. Broken Country

Set in 1950s England, this suspenseful love story unfolds into a tale of murder and secrets. The narrative intricately weaves romance with mystery, keeping readers engaged as the characters navigate love and danger.

  • Written by :Clare Leslie Hall.
  • Suitable For:Fans of historical fiction that combines romance and mystery, offering a gripping and atmospheric read.

1. Promise me Sunshine

A heartwarming romance about two strangers whose lives become unexpectedly intertwined after a mix-up at an airport. As they embark on an impromptu road trip together, they learn that sometimes, fate works in mysterious ways.

  • Written by:Cara Bastone.
  • Suitable For:Romance lovers who enjoy feel-good, quirky love stories with witty banter and emotional depth.
